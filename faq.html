What is ACEiT Group?

+

ACEiT Group is a leading IT infrastructure and technology solutions provider in Malaysia. We deliver comprehensive end-to-end network solutions through our three specialized subsidiaries:

Network Infrastructure (AceTeam Networks)

What network infrastructure solutions do you provide?

+

AceTeam Networks delivers comprehensive enterprise networking solutions including:

  • Wired & Wireless Networks: Campus-wide connectivity, Wi-Fi 6/6E deployment
  • Branch WAN Solutions: SD-WAN, MPLS, and hybrid connectivity
  • Network Access Control: ClearPass, 802.1X authentication, policy enforcement
  • Centralized Network Management: Cloud-managed infrastructure with AI insights
  • 5G Enterprise Networks: Private 5G deployment and integration
  • Data Centre Networking: Core switches, leaf-spine architecture

Do you work with specific network vendors?

+

Yes, we are authorized partners with leading network infrastructure vendors:

  • Aruba Networks (HPE): Enterprise wireless, switching, and SD-WAN
  • Cisco: Routing, switching, and data center solutions
  • Ruckus Networks: High-density wireless and SmartZone controllers
  • Extreme Networks: Campus and data center networking

Our partnerships ensure you receive certified solutions, warranty support, and access to the latest technologies.
