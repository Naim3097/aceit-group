2 Days to Launch: ACEiT is Coming

The Journey to ACEiT

For years, AceTeam Networks, AceTeam Digital, and AceTeam Connect operated as independent divisions, each excelling in their respective domains. Networks built infrastructure. Digital crafted experiences. Connect secured everything. Each division was strong on its own, delivering exceptional results to clients across Malaysia and the region.

But something was missing. In conversations with enterprise clients, a pattern emerged: they didn't want three vendors—they wanted one integrated partner who understood their complete IT ecosystem. They needed network infrastructure, digital collaboration solutions, and cybersecurity protection working seamlessly together, not in silos.

Why ACEiT? Why Now?

The acronym says it all: ACE in IT. We're not just combining divisions—we're unifying our vision, our expertise, and our commitment to being the best in enterprise technology. The launch on January 12, 2025, represents months of strategic planning, brand development, and operational integration to ensure that when ACEiT goes live, it's not just a name change—it's a complete transformation.

This isn't about corporate restructuring. It's about delivering what Malaysian enterprises have been asking for: a single, trusted partner capable of managing their entire IT infrastructure from network backbone to endpoint security, from unified communications to cloud solutions.

What This Means for Our Clients

One Point of Contact: Instead of coordinating between multiple divisions, clients will have a unified relationship manager who understands their complete IT environment and can mobilize resources across all three domains seamlessly.

Integrated Solutions: Network deployments will be designed with security from day one. Digital transformation projects will be built on robust infrastructure. Cybersecurity strategies will align with business objectives, not just technical requirements.

Faster Response Times: When issues arise, you won't be passed between departments. Our unified operations center can diagnose whether a problem is network, security, or application-related and deploy the right expertise immediately.

Our Partner Ecosystem

ACEiT brings together world-class technology partnerships across all domains. From Aruba Networks and Juniper for enterprise networking, to Palo Alto Networks and Fortinet for security, to Zoom and Poly for collaboration—we're backed by the industry's best.

These partnerships aren't just vendor relationships. They're strategic alliances that give our clients access to cutting-edge technology, preferential support, and enterprise-grade solutions that smaller providers simply cannot deliver.

The Team Behind ACEiT

Our strength isn't just in technology—it's in people. The ACEiT team includes Malaysia's first Aruba Certified Professional, certified cybersecurity specialists, and digital transformation experts who've delivered solutions for some of the region's largest enterprises.

We're ISO-certified, Microsoft-aligned, and built on a foundation of Malaysian talent that understands both global technology trends and local business realities. When you work with ACEiT, you're not outsourcing to a faceless vendor—you're partnering with experts who are invested in your success.

2 Days to Go

On January 12, 2025, ACEiT officially launches with a new website, unified service offerings, and an integrated approach to enterprise IT that Malaysia has never seen before. We're not promising perfection—we're promising partnership, expertise, and an unwavering commitment to being ACE in IT for every client we serve.

The countdown is on. The transformation is real. And we can't wait to show you what's coming. Visit us at ACEiT.asia starting January 12, and discover what it means to have one group, three divisions, and all IT working for you.
