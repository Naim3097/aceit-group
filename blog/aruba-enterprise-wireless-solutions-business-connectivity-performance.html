High-Performance Access Points

Aruba's access point portfolio delivers industry-leading performance with support for the latest wireless standards including Wi-Fi 6E and Wi-Fi 7, providing sufficient bandwidth and features to support demanding applications and high-density user environments.

Advanced antenna designs and beamforming technologies optimize signal coverage and quality while minimizing interference, ensuring consistent connectivity across diverse physical environments and usage scenarios.

Power over Ethernet support enables flexible installation without dedicated power infrastructure while advanced power management optimizes energy consumption and supports high-power features including dedicated security radios and enhanced transmit power.

Intelligent RF Management

Adaptive Radio Management (ARM) continuously monitors RF conditions and automatically adjusts channel assignments, power levels, and antenna configurations to optimize performance while mitigating interference from neighboring networks and non-Wi-Fi devices.

ClientMatch technology ensures that devices connect to optimal access points based on signal quality, capacity, and performance requirements rather than simply the strongest signal, improving overall network efficiency and user experience.

Enterprise Security Integration

Comprehensive wireless security features include WPA3 encryption, advanced authentication mechanisms, and integrated intrusion detection systems that protect against wireless-specific threats including rogue access points and man-in-the-middle attacks.

Integration with Aruba ClearPass provides identity-based access control that extends enterprise security policies consistently across wireless connections while supporting diverse device types and user authentication requirements.

Professional wireless security implementation from experienced security specialists ensures comprehensive protection while maintaining user experience and operational efficiency across enterprise wireless networks and guest access systems.

Scalable Management Platforms

Flexible management options including cloud-based Aruba Central and on-premises controllers provide scalable administration capabilities that grow with business requirements from small deployments to large enterprise networks spanning multiple locations.

Zero-touch provisioning enables rapid deployment of wireless infrastructure without on-site configuration expertise, reducing installation time and ensuring consistent policy application across all access points and locations.

Advanced Analytics and Insights

Comprehensive wireless analytics provide detailed visibility into network performance, user behavior, and application usage patterns that inform optimization decisions and support proactive network management strategies.

User experience monitoring tracks individual device and application performance, enabling identification of connectivity issues and performance bottlenecks that impact productivity and business operations.

IoT and Device Support

Specialized IoT capabilities including dedicated IoT radios, device profiling, and optimized connectivity protocols support diverse IoT ecosystems including sensors, cameras, building automation systems, and industrial monitoring equipment.

Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) integration enables location services, asset tracking, and proximity-based applications that leverage wireless infrastructure for business value beyond basic connectivity.

High-Density Environment Optimization

Advanced technologies including MU-MIMO, OFDMA, and coordinated beamforming enable efficient spectrum utilization in challenging high-density environments such as auditoriums, conference centers, and open office spaces with hundreds of concurrent users.

Load balancing and band steering capabilities distribute users across available resources to prevent individual access points from becoming performance bottlenecks during peak usage periods.

Guest Access and Network Segmentation

Sophisticated guest access management provides secure internet connectivity for visitors while maintaining network segmentation that protects corporate resources and ensures compliance with security policies.

Customizable captive portals enable branded guest experiences with flexible authentication options including social media login, SMS verification, and sponsor-based approval workflows.

Outdoor and Industrial Solutions

Ruggedized outdoor access points provide weatherproof operation for parking areas, courtyards, warehouses, and industrial facilities while maintaining enterprise-grade performance and security features.

Industrial wireless solutions address challenging RF environments including manufacturing facilities, distribution centers, and outdoor installations with specialized antennas and environmental protection.

Healthcare and Vertical Solutions

Healthcare-specific wireless solutions include features designed for medical environments such as HIPAA compliance, interference mitigation for medical devices, and specialized security policies for patient data protection.

Education, hospitality, and retail-specific features address unique requirements including high-density support, guest access management, and integration with industry-specific applications and systems.

Performance Optimization and QoS

Advanced quality of service capabilities prioritize business-critical applications while ensuring fair resource allocation across all users and devices, maintaining performance during network congestion periods.

Application visibility and control features enable network administrators to identify and optimize performance for specific applications while managing bandwidth consumption across the wireless network.
